# Registry Listing — Danish Energy Regulation MCP

Danish energy sector regulatory intelligence covering four regulators: Energistyrelsen (energy policy), Forsyningstilsynet (tariff regulation), Energinet (grid codes), and Sikkerhedsstyrelsen (electrical and gas safety). 6 tools with `dk_energy_` prefix for searching regulations, grid codes, and tariff decisions. All data in Danish with full-text search.

**Install:** `npx @ansvar/danish-energy-regulation-mcp`

**HTTP:** `POST https://mcp.ansvar.eu/danish-energy-regulation/mcp`
